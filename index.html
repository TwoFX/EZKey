Work in progess

This is currently about as alpha as it gets. There are lots of awesome features to come and the program still has lots of problems. Here's a list of what's coming in the future:

  • Additional keys: Space, Alt/Ctrl, Shift, Caps Lock, Tab etc.
  • Customization: customizable colors (foreground, background, text), size, space between keys, round edges, keyboard layout
  • Labeled keys (Customizable color, font, size etc.)
  • Many things I cannot think of at the moment
